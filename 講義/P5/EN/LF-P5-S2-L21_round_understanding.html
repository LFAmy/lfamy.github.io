II.Core Knowledge ＋ Worked Examples
⭕ The structure of a circle - center (O), radius (r), diameter (d)
The center O is the center point of the circle. Radius r is the distance from the center of the circle to the circumference. The diameter d=2r passes through the center of the circle and connects both ends of the circle. Red dashed line = diameter.

🪤 Trap detonation example - relationship between radius and diameter
Example 1: The radius of a circle is 5 cm, what is its diameter?
❌ Common mistakes (40% students)
d = 5 ÷ 2 = 2.5 cm
Put d = r ÷ 2, reversing the relationship
✅ Correct solution
d = 5 × 2 = 10 cm
The diameter is twice the radius: d = 2r

Knowledge point 2: Drawing a circle - the use of compasses 🔴 SSPA implementation
① The two legs of the compassare: one leg is thepin(fixed at the center of the circle), and the other leg is thepencil leg(draw the circumference).
② Compass opening distance = radius(not diameter! This is the most frequent error).
③ If the diameter is given in the question, you must first calculater = d ÷ 2and then adjust the compass opening.
④ Steps to draw a circle: ① Determine the center of the circle ② Measure the radius and adjust the opening of the compass ③ Pin the center of the circle with the pin ④ Draw the circumference by going around with the foot of the pencil.
⑤ When drawing a circle, keep the compass vertical to the paper and do it in one go. Do not change the opening midway.

Knowledge point 4: Circle in life 🟡 Application
① Why are wheels round?The distance from the center of the circle to the ground is constant (=radius), and the height of the axle remains unchanged when driving, making it smooth and not bumpy. Square wheels make the car bounce up and down.
② Clock hands |||SEP|||: The pointer starts from the center of the circle, and the trajectory traveled by the needle tip is circular. Pointer length = radius of circle.CD/Coin/Coaster |||SEP|||: Round shape without sharp corners, easy to make, carry and use.
③ Why are manhole covers round?The circular manhole cover will not fall into the wellhead in any direction (the diameter is the same), and the circle is evenly stressed.
