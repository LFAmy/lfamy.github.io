Knowledge Point 1: Multi-digit numbers and reading and writing rulesрҹ”ҙ SSPA
в‘  Digit sequence (from right to left):Units вҶ’ Tens вҶ’ Hundreds вҶ’ Thousands вҶ’ Tens of Thousands вҶ’ Hundreds of Thousands вҶ’ Millions вҶ’ Tens of Tens вҶ’ Hundreds of Hundreds
в‘Ў Hong Kong sectioning method (four digits per digit):From right to left, draw one digit for every four digits: one level | ten thousand level | billion level
в‘ў Reading rules:First read "Level 100" вҶ’ then read "Level 10,000" вҶ’ finally read "Level 1". Each level is internally read as thousands and hundreds of ten
в‘Ј The reading method of 0 - the most important rule:The 0 at the end of each level is not read; the consecutive 0s in the middle of each level only read one "zero"
