🪤 Trap detonation example 3 (the most important demonstration in this class)
calculate:23 ÷ 14 = ？
❌ Common mistakes (60% students)
23 × 14 = 212 = 16
Forgot to reverse the divisor of 14! Treat it directly as a multiplication sign.
✅ Correct solution
23 × 41 = 83 = 223
÷14 → ×41 (the fraction after the division sign must be reversed!)
🧠 Tip: "Invert the division sign and turn it into a multiplication sign. The numerator is multiplied by the numerator and the denominator is multiplied by the denominator. The answer must be simplified!"

Knowledge Point 3: Division with Mixed Fractions🔴 SSPA Advanced
Standard three-step method:
① mixed fraction → improper fraction(integer × denominator + numerator)
② Reverse the divisor after the division sign, the division sign becomes the multiplication sign
③ Multiply the numerator by the numerator and the denominator by the denominator→ Reduction → Convert the improper fraction to a mixed fraction

#common errorCorrect Approach
1÷integer is treated as×integer：34 ÷ 2 = 64÷2 = ×12, not ×2! The reciprocal of an integer is 1/integer
2÷Fraction forgetting to reverse the divisor：23 ÷ 14 = 212÷14 = ×41, only the one after the division sign is inverted!
3Reverse the previous fraction as well：23÷45=32×54Only fractions after ÷ are inverted! The front is unchanged, the back is reversed.
4The answer is not reduced：612as the final answerMust be reduced to the simplest fraction (numerator and denominator ÷ HCF)
5Improper fractions are not converted to mixed fractionsNumerator > Denominator → Convert into mixed numbers (subject to the requirements of the division test)
6Mixed fractions are divided directly by ÷without conversion: 1½÷2You must first convert to an improper fraction (1½ = 3/2), and then follow the division steps
7Application question "number of copies vs. each copy" confusionTotal amount ÷ amount per serving = number of servings; total amount ÷ number of servings = amount per serving. Distinguish clearly!
