2. Core Knowledge (15 min)
WHY BOX: Why learn conversions?
Fractions, decimals, and percentages are three languages for the same thing! 1/2 = 0.5 = 50% — they all mean 'half'.
SSPA tests your ability to switch between these forms instantly. Master the conversions and you master ~15% of the exam!
Memory aid: 1/4=0.25=25%, 1/2=0.5=50%, 3/4=0.75=75% — memorize these base conversions!
💡 Conversion Triangle: Fraction → Decimal = Numerator ÷ Denominator. Decimal → % = ×100 add %. % → Fraction = ÷100 simplify.
📝 Example 1
Convert 3/5 to decimal and percentage
Solution: 3÷5=0.6, 0.6×100=60%
📝 Example 2
Which is largest: 0.45, 40%, or 2/5?
Solution: 0.45=45%, 40%=40%, 2/5=0.4=40%. Answer: 0.45 (45%)
📝 Example 3
A store has 40% off. Express as fraction in simplest form.
Solution: 40% = 40/100 = 2/5
