2. Core Knowledge (15 min) — 核心知識精講
WHY BOX: Why learn decimal division?
Decimal division is everywhere in real life!
Splitting a $15.60 bill among 3 friends → 15.60 ÷ 3
Finding price per kg: 2.5kg of apples cost $22.50 → 22.50 ÷ 2.5
Key insight: Decimal division = integer division + decimal point alignment. The decimal point in the quotient must align with the decimal point in the dividend!
💡 Golden Rule: The decimal point in the quotient (answer) must align with the decimal point in the dividend. After placing the decimal point, divide as normal integers. Add zeros to the remainder when needed.
📝 Example 1: Decimal ÷ Integer (小數÷整數)
Calculate: 7.2 ÷ 3
Step-by-step:
1) 7 ÷ 3 = 2 remainder 1 → Write 2. (decimal point aligned!)
2) Bring down 2 → 12 ÷ 3 = 4 → Write 4
3) Answer: 2.4
Check: 2.4 × 3 = 7.2 ✓
📝 Example 2: Adding zeros to continue
Calculate: 5 ÷ 4
Step-by-step:
1) 5 ÷ 4 = 1 remainder 1 → Write 1.
2) Add 0 to remainder → 10 ÷ 4 = 2 remainder 2 → Write 2
3) Add 0 again → 20 ÷ 4 = 5 → Write 5
4) Answer: 1.25
Check: 1.25 × 4 = 5 ✓
📝 Example 3: Decimal ÷ Decimal (SSPA Killer)
Calculate: 3.6 ÷ 0.4
Strategy: Convert to integer division!
1) Multiply both by 10: 36 ÷ 4
2) 36 ÷ 4 = 9
3) Answer: 9
Key: a ÷ b = (a×10) ÷ (b×10) = (a×100) ÷ (b×100)... shift decimal points together!

4. Common Mistakes — 易錯點總結
#MistakeCorrection
1Forgetting to align decimal point in quotientAlways align quotient decimal point with dividend decimal point
2Not adding zeros when remainder existsAdd 0 and continue dividing until remainder = 0 or repeating pattern found
3Dividing decimal by decimal without shiftingAlways shift decimal points together: multiply both by same power of 10
5. Solution Strategy Card — 解題策略卡
4-Step Method for Decimal Division:
1) Align — Place decimal point in quotient aligned with dividend
2) Divide — Divide as whole numbers from left to right
3) Extend — Add zeros to remainder and continue
4) Check — Multiply: quotient × divisor = dividend ✓
