P1-P2 Special Teaching Method (From Master Curriculum)

Shorter Lecture: 18 minutes (not 20). Young children have shorter attention spans.
Extended Warm-Up: 12 minutes. Use games, stories, physical objects.
Play-Based Learning: 15 minutes. incorporate puzzles, matching games, digital manipulatives.
No Pressure: Never say "wrong." Say "let us try another way!" Building confidence > perfect answers.
Parent Partnership: Weekly at-home activity suggestions. Math in daily life.
