HK 4-Step: Circle, Draw, List, Check

1
Circle Keywords - Circle key numbers and question words
HK method: Find what the question asks! Circle: total, remaining, difference!
2
Draw Diagram - Convert text to picture or number line
HK method: Draw it out! Words lie, pictures do not. Rectangle? Draw one!
3
List Calculation - Step by step, vertical or horizontal
HK method: Line up working! Every step clear, no skipping. Messy = wrong!
4
Check Answer - Reverse calculation / estimation
HK method: Check! Does it make sense? Person volume is not 1000 cubic meters!

Affinity Training 親切感培訓 (Most Important!)

5 Rapport Techniques（親切故事建立法）

1: 30-Second Pre-Class Icebreaker
First 30 sec every lesson: ask non-math question. "What lunch today?" "Watch TV yesterday?" Students need: "This teacher is human, not machine."
2: Name + Praise
Call every student by name: "Xiao Ming, give it a try! I know you can." When correct: "Brilliant! You avoided T6 trap!" Children need to feel seen.
3: Share Your Own Failures
Once per lesson: "I used to fall for T4 trap too! 408/2 I answered 24!" Teacher vulnerability = "mistakes not a big deal."
4: Use Student Language
Not "numerator/denominator" but "top number, bottom number". Enter their world: "This question is like a Boss fight!" Children think in concrete language.
5: One Humor Moment Every 10 Minutes
Exaggerated expressions, funny analogies, deliberate mistakes. Laughter = relaxation = more willing to try = better learning.

HK Calculation Methods (Must Teach!)

1
Vertical Form
Standard HK primary format. All 4 operations use vertical.
"Write in vertical form! Align units/tens/hundreds! Misaligned = wrong!"
2
Short Division (LCM/HCF)
Standard method for LCM and HCF.
"Use short division! Divide together until no common factor!"
3
Cross Multiplication
For fraction comparison and common denominators.
"Cross multiply! TLxBR, TRxBL. Bigger result = bigger fraction!"
4
Estimation Check
HK exams require estimation.
"After calculating, estimate! 398x51~400x50=20000. Way off? Wrong!"
