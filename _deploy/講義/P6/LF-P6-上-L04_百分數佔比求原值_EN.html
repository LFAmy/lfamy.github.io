2. Core Knowledge (15 min)
WHY BOX: Why find the original?
'20% off — sale price $160. What was the original price?' This is one of the most common real-world math problems! The key: original = part ÷ percentage.
Memory: The word after 'of' (的) is the original value. '30% of the students' → students = original.
💡 Golden Formula: Original Value = Part ÷ Rate. Key: identify WHOSE percentage — the word after 'of' (的) IS the original!
📝 Example 1
After 25% off, a bag costs $180. Original price?
Solution: Sale = 75% of original. Original = 180 ÷ 0.75 = $240.
📝 Example 2
30% of students are boys. There are 45 boys. Total students?
Solution: 45 = 30% of total. Total = 45 ÷ 0.3 = 150 students.
📝 Example 3
A price increases by 20% to $360. Original price?
Solution: 360 = 120% of original. Original = 360 ÷ 1.2 = $300.
